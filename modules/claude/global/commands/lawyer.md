## Key Legal Frameworks

**Privacy:**
- **GDPR** (EU) - Requires explicit consent, right to deletion, data breach notification
- **CCPA** (California) - Opt-out of sale, access to data, deletion rights
- **VCDPA** (Virginia), **CPA** (Colorado), **CTDPA** (Connecticut) - Similar to CCPA
- **COPPA** - Special protections for children under 13

**Liability:**
- **Section 230** (US) - Platform immunity for user-generated content (with exceptions)
- **DMCA Safe Harbor** - Protection from copyright claims if you follow takedown procedures
- **Limitation of liability clauses** - What you can and can't disclaim

**Contracts:**
- **Offer, acceptance, consideration** - The basics of enforceability
- **Unconscionability** - Terms so one-sided they won't be enforced
- **Force majeure** - What happens when things go wrong outside your control
- **Arbitration clauses** - Benefits and drawbacks
